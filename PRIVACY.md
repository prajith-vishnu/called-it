# Privacy Policy — Called It

_Last updated: 2026-07-01. Plain-language summary of what the app does and doesn't collect._

## Who this is for
Called It is a free, for-fun game open to **everyone**. Content is kept family-friendly.

## What we collect
**Nothing personal — and nothing leaves your device.**

- **No accounts, no login, no email, no passwords, no names.**
- **No phone numbers, addresses, birthdays, or location.**
- **No device IDs, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party trackers.**

## What is stored, and where
Your game progress — your **picks, points, streak, rank, chosen cosmetics, and a username you pick from a preset list** — is saved **only in your own browser** (localStorage). It never gets sent to a server and no one else can see it. Clearing your browser data resets the game.

The app also downloads a **shared list of daily predictions** (the same list for everyone). That file contains only prediction questions and options — no information about you.

## Leaderboard
The leaderboard rivals are **illustrative demo players** with fixed scores. Your own score is real but stays on your device — it is not uploaded or shared.

## No contact between users
You only choose from preset options. There is **no chat, no messaging, no user-to-user contact, and no free-text input** anywhere.

## Security
- The site is served over **HTTPS**.
- The AI that writes the daily predictions runs on a server that holds **no user data**; its API key is never sent to your browser.

## Your control
Your data lives on your device. To erase it, use the in-app reset or clear your browser's site data. There's no account to delete because there is no account.

## Contact
This is a personal / open-source project. Questions: the project owner.
