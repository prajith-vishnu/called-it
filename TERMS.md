_Last updated: 2026-07-01. Plain-language terms for this portfolio prototype._

1. **Who can use it.** Open to everyone — it's a free, family-friendly game.
2. **It's a game for fun.** Called It is a free, for-fun prediction game. Points, streaks, ranks, badges, and leaderboard position are **cosmetic status only**. They have **no monetary or real-world value**, cannot be bought, sold, traded, or redeemed for anything, and nothing here is gambling.
3. **Predictions are entertainment.** Questions and AI-generated content are for fun and may be wrong. Nothing here is advice of any kind.
4. **Accounts are optional and minimal.** Guest progress stays in your own browser. An account is just a username and a hashed password — no email, no personal data — as described in the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md). You're responsible for keeping your password safe (there is no email, so there is no reset).
5. **Acceptable use.** Pick options, don't attack the service. Usernames must be appropriate for all ages (they're filtered, and inappropriate accounts may be removed). No cheating, scraping, flooding, or attempting to bypass rate limits — scores are computed server-side and abuse may result in removal from the leaderboard.
6. **Data.** Your data is handled as described in the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md): guest progress stays on your device; accounts store only a username, a hashed password, and your picks.
7. **No warranty.** The app is provided "as is," without warranties. It may change or go offline at any time.
