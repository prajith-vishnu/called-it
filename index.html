Privacy & Terms

Free & for everyone. Called It is a for-fun prediction game. Points, ranks, and cosmetics are status only — no money, nothing for sale, nothing redeemable.

Accounts are optional and minimal. Playing as a guest stores everything in this browser only. If you create an account, we store exactly: a username you choose, a securely hashed password (never the password itself), and your picks. No email, no name, no location, no analytics, no third-party trackers.

Guest progress stays on your device. Guest picks, points, streaks, and cosmetics live in this browser's localStorage. Clearing browser data resets guest progress.

The leaderboard shows only your username, score, and streaks. Don't put personal information in your username.

No user-to-user contact. There is no chat, no messaging, and no free-text visible to other players besides usernames, which are filtered.

Daily predictions are AI-generated on a schedule and safety-filtered before anyone sees them; the app only ever reads a cached list.
